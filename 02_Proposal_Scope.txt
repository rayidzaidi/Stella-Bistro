# Proposal & Scope of Work
Project: Website Design & Development
Prepared For: Stella Bistro
Date: March 6, 2026

Executive Summary
The goal of this project is to provide Stella Bistro with a premium, high-performing website that reflects its elevated rooftop dining experience. The website is designed to drive direct consumer action: WhatsApp reservations, easy location finding, and clear menu browsing.

Project Goals
1. Increase direct table reservations via WhatsApp integration.
2. Improve customer experience with a mobile-first, highly readable digital menu.
3. Drive foot traffic by making the Tariq Road location easily discoverable via Google Maps.
4. Capture leads for private events, birthdays, and catering.

Scope & Deliverables
The website will include the following core sections and features:
* Home Page: Hero section with parallax scrolling, "The Stella Experience" highlights, and quick calls-to-action.
* Menu System: A "Menu Highlights" section on the homepage and a dedicated "Full Menu" page with categories and prices.
* Gallery: A visual grid showcasing food and rooftop ambiance.
* Reviews: Integration of existing 5-star Google Reviews.
* Events: A contact form tailored for event inquiries (birthdays, corporate, etc.) that routes directly to your WhatsApp.
* Contact & Location: Embedded Google Map, address details, Call button, and WhatsApp chat button.
* Technical: Mobile responsive design, fast loading speeds, hosting, and domain setup.

What is NOT Included
To keep expectations clear, the following are not included in this base scope:
* Professional food or architectural photography (client to provide photos).
* Paid advertising management (Facebook/Google Ads).
* A custom, built-in e-commerce ordering system with a payment gateway (we use WhatsApp/Foodpanda routing instead).

Content Responsibilities
* Provider (Me): Website structure, design, coding, animations, map integration, and layout.
* Client (Stella Bistro): Official phone numbers, WhatsApp numbers, email (if any), final menu prices/text, and high-quality photos of the food and restaurant. 

Timeline & Milestones
* Target: 5 days to "Go-Live" after receiving all required content and the initial deposit.

Pricing & Payment Schedule

* Payment Terms: 50% deposit to start (PKR 40,000) / 50% before the site officially goes live (PKR 40,000).
