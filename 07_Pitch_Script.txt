# 90-Second Pitch Script (In-Person)

You: "Hi, I know you’re busy running the kitchen and the floor, so I’ll be quick. My name is [Your Name]. I noticed Stella Bistro is doing amazing things—the rooftop vibe is incredible and your Google reviews are fantastic."

Owner: "Thank you."

You: "I also noticed that finding your full menu online and booking a table quickly on WhatsApp isn't as seamless as it could be. I actually took the liberty of building a complete, working concept website for Stella Bistro just to show you what’s possible."

Owner: "We didn’t ask for a website."

You: "I know! That’s exactly why I built it first—so you could see it without any risk or obligation. I just wanted to show you how easily we can route customers from Google Maps directly to your WhatsApp for reservations, and give your late-night crowd a mobile-friendly menu they can easily read. Take a look at this QR code..." (Hand them the 1-page leave-behind). "...scan that and you'll see the working site on your phone right now."

(Let them look for a few seconds).

You: "Everything is basically done. To finalize it and make it officially yours, I just need your official WhatsApp number, some of your own food photos, and a 50% deposit. We can have this live and generating direct reservations in 5 days. What do you think of the design?"
