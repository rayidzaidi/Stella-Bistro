# Website Care & Maintenance Plan

Your website is a digital storefront. Just like a physical restaurant needs cleaning, security, and updates, your website needs ongoing maintenance to run smoothly. 

Monthly Investment: PKR 20,000 / month

What Your Plan Includes:
* Menu Updates: Need to change a price, add a new dish, or remove an old one? Just text me. (Fair use applies: up to 5 minor updates per month).
* Photo Updates: Swap out old ambiance photos or feature a new seasonal dish on the homepage.
* Banner Changes: Add a banner for Eid, Ramadan specials, or a limited-time 4 AM promo.
* Monthly Hosting: Ongoing premium server space to keep the site fast.
* Domain Renewal Setup: ensuring you never lose your web address.
* Security & Backups: Regular monitoring to keep the site safe from hackers and regular backups so your data is never lost.
* Uptime Monitoring: Ensuring the site is online when your customers are searching for you at 2 AM.

Normal Turnaround Time:
Most standard requests (changing a price, updating a photo, altering business hours) are completed within 24 to 48 hours.

What is NOT Included:
This maintenance fee is designed for upkeep and minor tweaks. It does not include:
* Complete website redesigns or adding entire new complex sections.
* Social media management (posting to your Instagram/Facebook).
* Managing paid Google or Facebook Ads.
